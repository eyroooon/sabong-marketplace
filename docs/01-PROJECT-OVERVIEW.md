# SabongMarket - Philippine Gamefowl Marketplace

## Project Overview

**SabongMarket** is a dedicated e-commerce marketplace for buying and selling gamefowl (roosters and hens) for the regulated sabong (cockfighting) industry in the Philippines. Think of it as the "Lazada/Shopee for Gamefowl" — a trusted platform connecting breeders, sellers, and enthusiasts.

---

## Vision

To become the #1 trusted online marketplace for gamefowl in the Philippines, providing a safe, transparent, and convenient platform for the sabong community.

## Mission

- Connect verified breeders and sellers with serious buyers nationwide
- Provide transparency through verified listings, ratings, and bloodline documentation
- Ensure secure transactions with escrow-based payment protection
- Build a community hub for the Philippine sabong industry

## Regulatory Compliance

### Philippine Laws to Consider
- **PD 449 (Cockfighting Law of 1974)** - Regulates cockfighting in the Philippines
- **RA 10532 (E-Commerce Act)** - Online business regulations
- **RA 10173 (Data Privacy Act)** - User data protection
- **DTI Registration** - Business registration requirements
- **BIR Registration** - Tax compliance for marketplace transactions
- **Animal Welfare Act (RA 8485, amended by RA 10631)** - Ensure humane treatment standards

### Platform Compliance Measures
- Seller ID verification (government-issued ID)
- Farm/breeder registration documentation
- Animal health certificate requirements for listings
- Age-appropriate content policies
- Transaction reporting for tax compliance

1. **Niche Focus** - No dedicated platform exists; sellers currently use Facebook groups
2. **Trust & Verification** - Verified breeders, bloodline documentation, escrow payments
3. **Bloodline Tracking** - Unique feature for gamefowl pedigree/lineage records
4. **Community Features** - Forums, breeder profiles, event calendars
5. **Mobile-First** - Built for the 78%+ of Filipinos who shop on mobile
6. **GCash/Maya Native** - Seamless payment with the wallets Filipinos actually use
